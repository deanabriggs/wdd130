Start a Project

So that you know what to expect and to help speed up the process of getting you online, here are some important questions for you to answer and information to help you get started.

Purpose

Why a website? What is the primary purpose of a webpage for you?

  • For more new business?
  • To provide a resource for existing customers?
  • Internal business use?
  • Or something else?

Who is your customer?

  • Are your customers parents of elementary kids, adults, college students, other businesses, etc.?
  • Do serve local, regional, national, international customers?

Content

Gather Your Content

This is the important information you want on your website. You will need to come up with and provide, the information you want to share keeping in mind your website purpose and audience.

Purpose/Objective of Your Business

This could be a mission statement, slogan, or moto. Consider what sets you apart from your competition. Also think of key words that people might use to search for you online.

About Your Business

Share about your business history, the ownership, experience or eductions in your trade. What licensing, certifications, insurance or other relevant credentials do you have that would be appealing to your customers?

Contact Information

Don't forget to provide the basics: business name, address, phone, email, operating hours, service area, Facebook or LinkedIn page.

Recommendations

Collect any reviews or testimonials that you might want to include.

Resources

Links to important sites such as Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.

Style

The Look

Go to our "Design Ideas" page to get ideas and find resources for color schemes, font formats, and more.

Design Ideas

Layout

How it Fits Together

We’ll setup some time to discuss how you want to pull it together. Once we have all the important elements for your website...

  • We’ll start a rough layout of how it can look and function using the content you put together.
  • We’ll create an outline of the information together so you can decide on the primary purpose of each page and what content will go where.
  • We’ll discuss placement of these different components and how they might be laid out. We might look at some existing websites that you like for some ideas and a point of reference on structure.

Draft

At this phase, I'll go to work!

  • Setting up your URL (i.e. www.yourbusiness.com)
  • Setting up your web hosting to store your web data.
  • Importing and formatting pictures you've provided.
  • Drafting a wireframe of your layout for you to review and approve.

Review

Review & Adjust

With the digital wireframe completed that includes your layout, colors, and fonts, we will sit down again to discuss any additions or changes that you want made.

Build

Now the real fun begins!

  • Structuring your website to mirror the wireframe and include the pictures and links that are pertinent to your site and add all your content.
  • Formatting your website with your color schemes and fonts.
  • Optimizing your website to be found using the keywords you provided.

Go Live

It's Done!

You’ll have an opportunity to review the completed website beforehand and make a few minor changes if desired. Then we will activate your site and your business will be online!

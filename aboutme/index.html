About Me

We live in Roseville, CA surrounded by a lot of family. We have 7 children (3-his, 1-mine, 3-ours) that are all grown, plus 3 grandchildren.

I enjoy serving people and spending time with my family. I like to play games, do crafts, project around the house, and go on small adventures.

I was raised in the church, though my path had a few curves in the road. I love the Lord and value being a member of His church.
